The role

Senior AI Engineer

What you're actually building

  • Backend systems that power agentic AI products at scale — not UI, not tooling for humans
  • Data pipelines that feed ML models the right training signal from billions of real customer interactions
  • Model-serving infrastructure that makes AI reliable in production, not just in notebooks
  • The scaffolding for Klaviyo's agentic architecture — the shared patterns other teams will build on
  • A culture of ownership: you ship, you learn, you raise the bar

How you'll spend your time

  • Designing async processing pipelines and distributed systems — Kafka, Celery, SQS, Redis at scale
  • Building and shipping generative AI and agentic applications into production environments
  • Creating evals — human and automated — that tell you when your AI is actually working
  • Collaborating directly with PMs, ML engineers, and data scientists on customer-facing products
  • Influencing architecture decisions on AWS and Kubernetes — not just executing someone else's plan
